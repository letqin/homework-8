About Me

My name is Quinn Ryan and I am training for a job in web development. In middle school, I discovered the developer tools native to Chrome. Modifying news headlines in the DOM was incredibly entertaining to me.

After high school, I went into mechanics immediately with Mercedes Benz. I was a mechanic for three and a half years fixing anything that came in (with frequent help from senior techs of course). I have decided that I value a more remote work experience. The skills I have gained from being a mechanic have taught me to look at a problem from every angle. The way I have been taught to work is centered around solving problems, coding is where I need to be.

Outside of coding I am more of an outdoor person. I particularly enjoy camping and hiking the most. I also enjoy sailing a sunfish sailboat, but have sailed a couple of larger ships.
